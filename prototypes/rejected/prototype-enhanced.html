What's the process for converting the models?
Paste your Pydantic models in the left panel and click Convert.
Are there limitations on supported types?
Common built-ins, nested models, enums, and datetime are supported in this prototype.
Can I copy the output easily?
Use the Copy action on the TypeScript panel to grab the generated interfaces.
Is my data sent anywhere?
No — all processing happens locally in your browser.
